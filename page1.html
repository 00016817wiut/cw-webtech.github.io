About Company

MAXIMA radio station conducts daily round-the-clock broadcasting on FM frequency-105.4 MHz. The language of broadcasting is Russian.
Broadcasting is carried out using modern high-tech studio equipment and transmitting equipment that meet international standards. Broadcasting coverage - Tashkent, Tashkent region.
The AS Soft format, in which MAXIMA radio broadcasts, includes the best works of world pop music of the 80s and 90s, and is designed for 25-60-year-old radio listeners. The percentage of music of the 80s and 90s refers to 40-30, 30 percent reflect the novelties of modern pop.
This format also includes news, reviews, short thematic sketches. Currently, the verbal broadcast occupies 10-15 percent of the broadcast network.
Much attention is paid on the air to social projects, including "Lullabies of the Planet", "Music of our Parents", social videos covering the most pressing aspects of our lives – environmental protection, care for the older generation, education of healthy youth. We try to avoid hackneyed cliches, so we do not always resort to standard lighting methods, because sometimes just a lullaby can stir up people's feelings and remind them of parents who need our support and care. And if this happens, then we are on the right track.
Thanks to the excellent mood from the very beginning of the day, members of our society working in various fields begin to increase their productivity, mothers and grandmothers will cook the most delicious lunch to the sounds of our wave, which will fill their beloved home with the aroma of warmth and comfort, drivers charging with positive energy of radio frequencies will improve the quality of service. But how much does it mean in our lives…
Over the years, such famous people as Alisher Kanaev, Lola Islamova, Leila Izmailova, D.Vilya (Vildan Gataulin) have worked on our wave. Each of them has brought something of their own to the development of our radio station and for this we are immensely grateful to them. But we do not stop on our way, and every year we are looking for new approaches in order to become even better, better and more interesting. And the love of our radio listeners is the best confirmation that we are on the right track.

Our Office

Welcome to our office in the vibrant city of Tashkent! Located in the heart of Uzbekistan, our team is privileged to work in this bustling metropolis. Tashkent offers a unique blend of rich history, stunning architecture, and a thriving cultural scene.

Situated within the business district, our modern office space provides a comfortable and dynamic environment for our team members. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and designed with productivity in mind, our office ensures that we deliver top-quality radio broadcasts to our listeners.

But it's not just the physical space that makes our office special. Our team is made up of talented and passionate individuals who are dedicated to bringing you the best radio content. From experienced radio hosts to creative scriptwriters, our diverse team works together to curate an engaging range of programs.

Beyond the office walls, Tashkent offers endless possibilities for inspiration and entertainment. Whether it's exploring the bustling Chorsu Bazaar, visiting the impressive Hast-Imam Square, or immersing yourself in the city's vibrant nightlife, there is always something exciting to discover in Tashkent.

We invite you to join us on our radio journey from our office in Tashkent. Tune in to our station and let us transport you to the heart of Uzbekistan, where our team works tirelessly to bring you the latest news, curated music playlists, and thought-provoking discussions.
